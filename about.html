About me

I am a backend developer with 2 years of experience, i love coding and use it as an artistic outlet. I especially enjoy working on algorithms, everything from their design, their performance and their useability. I prefer low-level languages like C as it forces me to stay sharp. My true love is numerical algorithms.

In my spare time i analyze Tabletop RPG systems, to get a better understanding of their mechanics and their inner statistical work. This has lead me to develop dicechanics, which is a library i use on an almost daily basis. I love feeling like i am improving, and this feeling is a great source of motivation, from programming to cooking to illustration.
